Overview
The General Coffee Coordinator (GCC) has operated from the manufacture of food to the design and the overall coffee business.   Starting with the Barista Training and Studio Community Space 'Coffee Workroom' in 2004, GCC has grown to become a world-class barista education and cafe consulting company. It has imported all the products from the world's leading coffee maker Cafe's Richard and the coffee machine Kees Van der Westen and is also the high-end coffee machine Mavam with exclusive products from Aremde and the U.S.   I was in charged of web design parts. The client wanted web page to be luxurious and simple style based on the company image. So that I chose three main colours black, white, and gold. Fonts size and gold colour to emphasis the keywords for each section. Using interactions to draw some interest in menu, previous, next, and top icon button.
